THE BEST GLUTEN FREE FOCACCIA EVER!

INGREDIENTS:
  1. Nutrifree Bread Flour 320 gr
  2. Nutrifree Whole wheat Bread Flour 80 gr
  3. Warm water 390 gr
  4. Yeast 3 gr
  5. Salt q.b.
  6. Olive oil 2 tbs
  7. Honey 1 tps
  8. Tomatoes/wurstel for optional topping
HOW TO:
  1. Place the water, the yeast and the honey in a large bowl
  2. Wait 10 minutes
  3. Then add gradually the flour, while mixing with a spoon
  4. When the dough is almost formed, add salt and olive oil
  5. Cover the dough with plastic wrap
  6. Let it rise for 4 hours at room temperature
  7. Move the dough on a work surface and let it sit for 40 minutes
  8. Meanwhile turn on the oven at 482 Â°F
  9. Eventually add coarse salt, more olive oil and the optional toppings
  10. Bake for about 30 minutes, until the surface is browned and crusty
